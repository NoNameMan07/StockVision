About Us

Welcome to Stock Price Prediction, where cutting-edge technology meets financial foresight. We are passionate about empowering individuals and investors with the tools to navigate the dynamic world of stock markets—both global and Indian—with confidence and clarity.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to demystify stock price movements and provide actionable insights through advanced predictive analytics. Whether you're tracking the performance of giants like Apple (AAPL) or exploring opportunities in Indian powerhouses like Tata Steel (TATASTEEL), we bring you reliable predictions to inform your financial decisions.

What We Do

At the heart of our platform lies a sophisticated LSTM (Long Short-Term Memory) neural network, meticulously trained to analyze historical stock data and forecast future prices. We integrate data from trusted sources like Yahoo Finance and the National Stock Exchange (NSE) via nsepy, ensuring comprehensive coverage of global and Indian markets. Enter a stock symbol, and within moments, you’ll receive a detailed prediction graph, accuracy metrics (MAE, RMSE, R², MAPE), and a tailored buy/sell recommendation—all presented in an intuitive, full-screen interface.

Why Choose Us?

We stand out by offering a seamless experience that caters to both novice investors and seasoned traders. Our system smartly detects whether a stock is Indian or international, displaying prices in ₹ or $ accordingly. With features like real-time training progress tracking and a robust fallback mechanism for data retrieval, we ensure you’re never left in the dark. Our predictions aren’t just numbers—they’re insights backed by science, designed to help you stay ahead of the curve.

Our Vision

We envision a world where financial decision-making is no longer a guessing game but a strategic process guided by data and innovation. By continuously refining our algorithms and expanding our market coverage, we aim to be your trusted partner in the ever-evolving landscape of stock trading.

Behind the Scenes

We are a team of data enthusiasts, machine learning experts, and financial aficionados united by a shared goal: to make stock prediction accessible, accurate, and actionable. Built with passion and powered by technology, this project is our contribution to a smarter investment future.
